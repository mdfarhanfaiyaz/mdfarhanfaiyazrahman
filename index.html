Research And Project

Undergraduate Thesis

  • Analysis Performance of Mechanical properties of Crumpled Graphene: A Molecular Dynamics Approach.

    • This study focused on performance on Crumpled Graphene(CG) under tensile and compressive loading. In this study elastic stiffness, stress vs strain, strain energy, fracture toughness of CG was observe at different crumpling radius. Result obtained for non porous CG was compared with porous CG at different degree of porosity.
    Software used: LAMMPS, MS EXCEL, OVITO, MATLAB, Tecplot 360

    • View Thesis

    CFDHT work

  • CFD study of MHD mixed convection in a quadrantal cavity with heat conducting rotating cylinder.

    • This study conducted inside a quadrantal enclosure with a heat conducting rotating Cylinder with magnetic field variation. Both clockwise and counterclockwise rotation of cylinder was considered. Variation of cylinder diameter performed. Thermal performance was studied keeping vertical wall cold, horizontal wall hot and the curve surface at adiabatic condition. Magnetic field was applied toward positive x direction. Average temperature and Nusselt Number was used to characterize heat transfer phenomena.
    Software used:Comsol MULTIPHYSICS, MS EXCEL, Tecplot 360, MATLAB
